My Favourite Ukrainian Song: Гей, соколи!

About "Гей, соколи!"

"Гей, соколи!" is traditionally considered a Ukrainian folk song, believed to be written by Tomasz Padura, though this attribution remains debated among scholars.1

The song is about a young Cossack saying farewell to his beloved and Ukraine, symbolizing themes of love, separation, and longing for the homeland. It has become an enduring cultural symbol, especially during times of conflict, like the recent Russian invasion of Ukraine, where it was used to boost morale and express solidarity

Про "Гей, соколи!"

"Гей, соколи!" — це популярна українська народна пісня з глибоким культурним значенням. Вона розповідає про тугу за рідним краєм і коханими...
